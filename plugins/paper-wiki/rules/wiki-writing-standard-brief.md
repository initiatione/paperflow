# Paper Wiki Writing Standard Brief

Read this brief for every Paper Wiki task. Load `wiki-writing-standard.md` before drafting, rewriting, materially repairing, relinking, or validating formal pages.

- Keep Obsidian syntax, paper-evidence semantics, and target-vault governance separate.
- Formal graph pages may live only under `references/`, `concepts/`, `derivations/`, `experiments/`, `synthesis/`, `reports/`, or `opportunities/`.
- `_paper_source/**`, legacy `_epi/**`, MinerU/raw files, snapshots, and staging/internal copies are evidence addresses, not formal graph nodes.
- `sources:` uses title-display Markdown links to canonical `_paper_source/raw/<slug>/paper.pdf` `obsidian://` URLs. Do not use internal wikilinks or plain PDF paths in `sources:`.
- MinerU Markdown is primary for formulas, notation, method context, and prose. Use PDF, figures, formula indexes, and image evidence only as fallback or visual checks.
- Material formal page rewrites are graph-aware transactions: inspect dependent pages, update relationships/provenance/tracking, and report Paper Source record readiness.
- After writes, repairs, relinks, redo, or staged deposition, run the route post-task check and report QMD refreshed, skipped, or failed with fallback.
